Website redesign

01 Introduction

What is Basset Hound rescue of Ontario ?

  • Dedicated to Basset Hounds only
  • NGO Based in Ontario
  • Educate about fostering
  • Adoption application
  • Talks about relinquished dogs

Problem Statement

A potential dog adopter who is interested in dog rescue seeks a user-friendly website with detailed profiles and responsive support to confidently navigate the adoption process, ensuring success in finding the perfect canine companion.

Solution

A revamped Basset Hound Rescue of Ontario website with streamlined navigation and a unified interface, featuring individual pages for each dog profile. The header's primary navigation is poised to significantly enhance user experience.
